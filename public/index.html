What to expect

Your first visit,
step by step.

01
History & Consultation
Your osteopath will listen carefully to your symptoms and medical history, asking questions to understand the full picture. Initial appointments are up to one hour — there's no rush.
02
Clinical Examination
A thorough assessment of posture, joint movement and function. Depending on your symptoms this may include neurological checks such as reflexes and blood pressure.
03
Treatment
Soft tissue massage, joint mobilisation, manipulation and exercise advice — tailored to your specific needs. If further investigation is appropriate, Clare will explain clearly and refer where needed.
04
Aftercare & Advice
Guidance on posture, exercises and lifestyle to support your recovery and reduce the likelihood of recurrence. Follow-up appointments are shorter and more focused.

Comfortable clothing recommended — leggings or shorts work well. Towels provided at the clinic.
