About Clare

Treating the person,
not just the symptom.

I've been practising osteopathy for over a decade, and the thing that still strikes me is how much a person's pain affects every part of their life — not just the physical. That's why I take time at the outset to really understand what's going on: your history, your lifestyle, what makes things better or worse.

Osteopathy works with the whole body. I use my hands to assess how your joints, muscles and tissues are moving — and where they're not. Treatment is gentle and targeted, drawing on a range of techniques from soft tissue work to joint mobilisation, depending on what you need.

I see patients at my clinic in Berkhamsted and in central London. Whether you're dealing with something acute that just came on, or a long-standing issue you've learned to live with, I'm here to help you get moving again.

What to expect

Your appointment,
step by step.

01
Case History
Your osteopath will take a full case history including your current symptoms, past medical history and general health. This helps build a complete picture before any examination begins.
02
Clinical Examination
A thorough assessment of your posture, movement and joint function. Depending on your symptoms this may include neurological testing such as reflexes, and a blood pressure reading. You are more than welcome to bring someone with you to your consultation.
03
Treatment
Soft tissue massage, articulation, stretching and where appropriate joint manipulation — all tailored to your specific needs. If further investigation is needed Clare will explain clearly and refer where appropriate.
04
Advice & Aftercare
Guidance on exercises, posture and lifestyle to support your recovery and help prevent recurrence. Follow-up appointments are more focused and typically shorter than the initial consultation.
What to Wear
Comfortable, loose-fitting clothing that allows easy access to the area being treated. Shorts or leggings are ideal. Towels are provided at the clinic if needed, so please don't worry.
Initial Consultations
Your first appointment is up to one hour. There is no rush — I want to understand your situation fully before we start. Subsequent appointments are usually 30–45 minutes.
Consent & Confidentiality
Your consent will be taken before any examination or treatment. All patient information is held in strict confidence under GDPR. You are under no obligation to continue with treatment at any point.
